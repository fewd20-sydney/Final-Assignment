The BeFranklin Project

There are great men who have lived and then there's Benjamin Franklin. No man can surpass Franklin in his ability to bring out the best in everyday life. A renaissance man in his own right, with an insatiable desire to change the world and live by virtues.

The Be Franklin project is an weekly Instagram feed dedicated to bringing those little nuggets of wisdom, knowledge and experience from one of the greatest men in history back to life. No more boring quote pasted on stock paintings of Benjamin Franklin. Each post is carefully crafted to bring out the beauty and pride in his words for the modern polymath hell
